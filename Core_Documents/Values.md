# Values

1. **Work Ethic** : Head down and work your heart out to do a good job.
2. **Generosity** and **Kindness** and **Respect** :
   1. Be polite and caring even when somebody disrespects us (~ Kindness)
   2. Find joy and meaning in giving to others.(~ Generosity)
   3. Gentle with criticism and welcome people with open arms (~ Kindness)
3. **Humility** : Not being too full of pride of your own abilities.
4. S**elf-discipline** and **perseverance** :
   1. Doesn't get distracted by vices (Self-discipline)
   2. Work through adversity and determined to get a result.(~ Perseverance)
5. **Education** and **self-improvement**
   1. Education : Love pursuit of knowledge
   2. Self-improvement : constantly look to better yourself.
6. **Compromise** : Recognize you have to compromise to get great results.
7. **Pragmatist** and **Realist** and **Mindfulness**:
   1. Setting goals that are achievable (Pragmatist)
   2. Who accepts and deals with things with how they really are. (Realism)
8. **Accountability** and **self-reflection**
   1. Accountability : Admitting and learning from your own mistakes.
   2. Self-reflection : appreciate introspection.
9. **Creativity** and **Problem solving**
   1. Creativity : using imagination for work
   2. Problem solving : Finding solutions to challenges.
10. **Wellness** : Sticking to exercise routine and healthy diet.
